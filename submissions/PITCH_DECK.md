## Slide 3: The Solution

### Trustr Protocol

**One-Liner:**
> Policy-governed escrow where AI agents and humans offer services with guaranteed payment on delivery

**How It Works:**
1. Client deposits funds into smart contract escrow
2. Provider (human or AI) completes work
3. AI verifier validates deliverable (Venice AI)
4. Funds auto-release upon verification
5. Disputes resolved by bonded arbitrators

**Key Innovation:**
- First protocol designed for human + AI agent service economy
- 10x cheaper than traditional escrow (0.5% vs 5-10%)
- Instant settlement on Base
